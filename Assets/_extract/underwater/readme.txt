Underwater sprites
------------------

Prerendered underwater sprites for use in deep sea 2D action games,
contains animated squid, stingray, starfish, rotating coin, gems, submarine.
Originally used in Tap Tap Submarine (html5, 2014)

You may use these graphics in personal and commercial projects.
Credit would be appreciated but is not mandatory.
